Self Test

Feeling down or on edge are common experiences for us all. When these feelings get in the way of life, it may be that you are experiencing depression or anxiety – or both. These self-tests ask some important questions that can help you take stock of where you are at so you can plan for feeling better sooner.



Our Mission

We are here to help you find quality information, help you to learn new skills, inform you about the nearby spiritual ceremonies and most importanly listen.
According to the National Institute of Mental Health and Neurosciences (NIMHANS), mental illnesses affect about 20% of the Indian population, with an estimated 150 million people in need of mental health care. However, only 10-12% of these individuals receive any form of treatment. The NMHP seeks to address this gap by training mental health professionals, strengthening mental health infrastructure, and promoting awareness and destigmatization of mental illnesses. NMHP
